At Casa Angelina we put breakfast on a pedestal

Breakfast at Casa Angelina is an elegant, unforgettable dining affair in and of itself, worthy of taking the time to savor. Our extensive, rotating buffet includes freshly baked bread, homemade cakes, savory tarts, locally picked fruits, honeys, jams, charcuterie, salmon, and salads. Our breakfast menu also includes specialty, made to order hot dishes

Beverage options include a vast array of tea blends, freshly squeezed fruit juices, mineral waters, and champagne.
