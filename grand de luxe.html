Grand De Luxe

our style statement

The beacon of our signature style.

Bright, airy and effortlessly elegant with furnishings and details by design greats such as Philip Starck and Gandia Blasco, the space is at once timeless and completely of the moment. With a private 30 sqm terrace showcasing Amalfi’s spectacular views, Grand De Luxe is the ideal spot to feel reconnected and reinvigorated.
