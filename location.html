location

an oasis of peace and quiet

A hidden gem on the Amalfi Coast

where serenity and style meet charm and authenticity

Perched amongst lemon and olive tree overlooking the glittering teal green waters of the Tyrrhenian, Casa Angelina is a pristine seaside sanctuary marked by refined luxury, remarkable service, and quintessential Italian hospitality. Our perfectly positioned enclave between the bustling towns of Amalfi and Positano, carved into the cliffside near the quaint fishing village of Praiano, offers guests a relaxed retreat steeped in history, romance, and culture, with a contemporary twist.

Juxtaposed with our host community’s old-world charm and beauty, Casa Angelina is a minimalist oasis that strikes the right balance of the past and present with its’ sophisticated, modern comforts and sleek, white structures set against the ancient, craggy coast.
