Wine Cellar

an award-winning wine selection

Our wine cellar is jam-packed with quality bottles from far and wide

At Un Piano Nel Cielo restaurant, our wine collection is expertly curated by our experienced sommelier with over 3,000 bottles and 600 wine labels. The collection is a recipient of the Wine Spectacular Award and consistently updated with new and diverse additions. Our shelves range from top vintages of renowned Italian producers to rare bottles from local regional winemakers accross the globe.

As a welcome gesture, and our way of saying “Benvenuto,” guests of Un Piano Nel Cielo are treated to a glass of Casa Angelina Champagne brut and rose, produced by French winemaker Pierre Mignon. Once dinner begins, our sommelier will help you navigate our vast selection of wine and bubbles or opt for a special tasting with customized wine pairings to accompany your meal.
