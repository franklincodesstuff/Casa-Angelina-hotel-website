Azure Suite

launched in April 2023

Design-driven luxury created by Italian interiors expert Paola Lenti.

Newly launched in 2023, the Azure Suite is a one-of-a-kind, immersive design experience curated by renowned interior designer Paola Lenti. Inspired by the suite’s stunning sea and sky vistas, Lenti created a modern haven for the most discerning of guests, bathed in shades of calming blue and white. This well-appointed suite celebrates Lenti’s bold, artistic eye and offers 50sqm of indoor space, including a comfortable living room, bedroom, ample bathroom, and walk-in closet. The Azure Suite is underscored by its’ expansive front terrace which boasts views of Positano and Capri.
